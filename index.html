About The Cozy Pub

Nestled in the heart of the city, The Cozy Corner Pub is more than just a place to eat and drink – it's a community hub. We've crafted a menu that celebrates classic pub fare with a modern twist, using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Our taps feature an ever-changing selection of the finest craft beers from independent breweries, alongside a curated list of wines and spirits. Whether you're catching a game, enjoying live music, or simply unwinding with friends, The Cozy Corner Pub is where good times are always on tap.
